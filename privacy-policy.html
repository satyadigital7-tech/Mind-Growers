Introduction

At Mind Growers Kiddoria, accessible from our website, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors and parents. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Mind Growers Kiddoria and how we use it.

Information We Collect

We collect information that you directly provide when enrolling a child or communicating via our forms, including:

  • Parent/Guardian Name, Phone number, and Email address.
  • Child's Name, Date of Birth, and Age.
  • Program choice (Preschool, Daycare, Academy, Activity Centre).
  • Health records and emergency contact details for confirmed enrollments.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in various ways, including to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our educational and daycare services.
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our curriculum activities.
  • Understand and analyze how you interact with our website.
  • Process admissions registrations and communicate school updates.
  • Coordinate student safety, transport routing, and medical emergencies.

Data Protection & Safety

Mind Growers Kiddoria secures children's offline and online records. Physical folders are kept in restricted office locations, and website contact inquiries are stored behind firewall-protected databases accessible only by authorized administration coordinators.

Contact Us

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at info@mindgrowerskiddoria.com.
