Our Inspiration

Rabindranath Tagore's Philosophy

"The highest education is that which does not merely give us information but makes our life in harmony with all existence."

At Mind Growers Kiddoria, we translate this philosophy into daily action. We believe that learning is not confined to textbooks or four walls. Instead, we nurture the child's mind in harmony with nature, encouraging exploration, sensory integration, and self-guided inquiry. Through gardening, playing in open-air mud pits, observing insects, and connecting with plants, our children discover their place in the wider universe and develop a natural respect for all living things.

Meet Our Founder

Childhood Blossoms Naturally

Mr. Varun J. Madhwani is the Founder & Director of Mind Growers Kiddoria, a nature-inspired preschool dedicated to nurturing young minds through meaningful, hands-on learning and holistic development. A graduate in Linguistics, he believes that every child is unique and learns best through curiosity, creativity, exploration, and real-life experiences. His vision is to create a joyful learning environment where children grow academically, emotionally, socially, morally, and spiritually while remaining deeply connected to nature.

Inspired by the educational philosophy of Rabindranath Tagore and guided by the divine blessings of his Kuldevi, Shree Hinglaj Mata, Mr. Varun Madhwani believes that true education flourishes when it is rooted in values, compassion, wisdom, and respect for all life. He sincerely prays that every child who becomes a part of the Mind Growers Kiddoria family is blessed with good health, happiness, knowledge, confidence, strong character, and a lifelong love for learning.

"May the divine blessings of Shree Hinglaj Mata guide every child at Mind Growers Kiddoria with wisdom, kindness, courage, and endless opportunities to learn, grow, and shine."

— Mr. Varun J. Madhwani B.A. in Linguistics | Founder & Director, Mind Growers Kiddoria

Parent Partnership

Growing Together: Home & School Collaboration

We believe that early development thrives when school and family are aligned. Our parent partnership initiative ensures you are fully connected with your child's milestones.

Regular Communication

Receive detailed daily digital logs outlining your child's sleep, nutrition, Montessori tasks, and happy moments.

Parent Meetings

Face-to-face updates and discussions to align individual learning tracks between school environment and home routines.

Family Engagement

Join hands for open days, nature exploration picnics, gardening workshops, and creative storytelling events on campus.

Progress Updates

Ongoing portfolio records tracking motor skills, sensory capabilities, independence levels, and emotional wellness indices.

Real-Life Learning

Parents and children collaborate on gardening, basic clay pottery, recycling cycles, and composting at our organic farm.
