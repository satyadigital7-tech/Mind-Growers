We accept children from 1 to 6 years old, categorized into Totplay (1-2 Years), Playgroup (2-3 Years), Nursery (3-4 Years), Junior KG (4-5 Years), and Senior KG (5-6 Years) to match their natural developmental milestones.

Our core academic programs run Monday to Friday, from 8:30 AM to 12:30 PM. For parents who opt for daycare, the school facility remains open with active care services up to 6:30 PM.

Yes, we have a safe, clean, and interactive daycare environment from 12:30 PM to 6:30 PM. Daycare kids receive fresh hot meals, structured nap times, sensory activities, and guided evening playground time.

To ensure personalized child mentoring, we maintain a close ratio of 1:8 in the Toddler level, and a 1:12 ratio in the Nursery, LKG, and UKG groups.

Following Tagore's vison of learning in freedom and nature, we conduct multiple classes outside. We focus on artistic expression, storytelling, direct contact with soil/plants, singing, and building a sense of harmony with the global environment.
