1. Enrollment & Admission

Admissions to Mind Growers Kiddoria are granted on a first-come, first-served basis, subject to availability. An admission is considered finalized only when all registration fees are paid, and valid supporting records (such as birth certificate, immunization charts, and photographs) are submitted.

2. School Timings & Pick-Up

Our academic program runs from 8:30 AM to 12:30 PM. Parents/Guardians must drop off children between 8:15 AM and 8:30 AM and arrange for prompt pickup at 12:30 PM. Children registered under our Daycare program can remain in the daycare section up to 6:30 PM.

3. Fee Structure & Payment Policies

Preschool and daycare fees are paid on a quarterly basis and are due by the 10th day of the start of each quarter. Late fees apply after the due date. Please note that registration charges, academic materials fees, and term tuition fees are strictly non-refundable.

4. Health & Medical Policy

For the safety of our entire community, children suffering from contagious illnesses (such as fever, severe cold, cough, or pink eye) must not be sent to school. We require parents to submit a physician's fitness certificate before the child returns after a major medical absence.

5. Code of Conduct & Communication

We expect mutual respect, open communication, and polite behavior between school teachers, administration staff, and parents. The school reserves the right to cancel enrollment in the event of persistent disruptive behavior or failure to comply with school administrative regulations.
