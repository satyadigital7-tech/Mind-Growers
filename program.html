Early Childhood Pathways

Preschool Stages & Breakdown

We offer structured, age-appropriate pathways designed to guide your child through sensory exploration, language acquisition, creative confidence, and school readiness.

1

Totplay

1 – 2 Years

Focuses on sensory exploration, motor skills, and strengthening parent-child bonding in a natural environment.

2

Playgroup

2 – 3 Years

Emphasizes early social skills, collaborative play, emotional expression, and child-led sensory learning.

3

Nursery

3 – 4 Years

Focuses on structured language acquisition, tactile Montessori activities, and creative confidence.

4

Junior KG

4 – 5 Years

Introduces basic concepts, numeracy, and pre-writing through natural, real-world explore activities.

5

Senior KG

5 – 6 Years

Prepares children for formal primary school while maintaining independence and creative confidence.
